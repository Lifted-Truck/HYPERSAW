[L0006] A wrapper's DEFAULTS are your host-facing surface — trace the delivery path before claiming host support | candidate | added: 2026-07-18 | tags: plugin-platform | lesson: ADR-036 shipped the claim "Live via the wrapper delivers MPE as note expressions with no further surface" — false on both legs. clap-wrapper's VST3 layer advertises only the note expressions in its _expressionmap, which defaults to PRESSURE-only behind a compile flag (CLAP_SUPPORTS_ALL_NOTE_EXPRESSIONS) that make_clapfirst_plugins never even forwards; and MPE can arrive as rotating member channels + per-channel 0xE0 CLAP_EVENT_MIDI, a type the shell ignored.
