Four LFOs — one each for morph, cutoff, chorus, saturation — ride one Kuramoto rotor. Each has its own rate, so they drift apart; K couples them: positive pulls their phases together (the four modulations lock into one shared motion), negative splays them to opposite corners of the cycle. The shape dropdown sets the waveform every LFO follows — sine, triangle, saw, square. Watch the dots orbit and the spokes breathe; turn K up and they gather into one hand sweeping the whole patch.
