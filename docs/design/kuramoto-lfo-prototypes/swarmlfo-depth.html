Axis 2 makes each voice's depth (its modulation amount) a swarm quantity. R→depth couples depth to coherence: positive = the swarm modulates harder as it locks (self-amplifying motion), negative = it backs off as it locks (self-erasing). Thin voices fades voices out one at a time — with the chorus routed and K high, this slides the emergent comb resonance between its pitch steps as the active tap count changes. Attrition sets how gradual each voice's fade is (soft crossfade vs abrupt). Route to chorus and sweep thin under high K to hear the resonance graduate.
