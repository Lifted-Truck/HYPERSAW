This is a coupled-LFO bank, not an audio swarm — the voices modulate, they don't sound. Axis 1 makes each voice's rate a swarm coordinate. K couples the rates: positive pulls every voice toward a common rate (the modulation locks into unison motion — Foxfire's chorus); negative splays the rates into an even interleave (the smoothest possible wash, denser than random). Tempo gravity pulls voice rates onto beat subdivisions — the shimmer snaps into a polyrhythmic grid against the BPM. Routing sends the same bank to a chorus (delay taps), a filter, and pan — turn up two at once to hear one population drive several destinations in lock-step.
