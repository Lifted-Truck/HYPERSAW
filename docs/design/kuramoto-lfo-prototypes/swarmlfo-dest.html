Axis 3 — the radical one. Each voice's destination (which of chorus / filter / pan / pitch it modulates) is now a swarm coordinate. J couples the destinations: positive converges every voice onto one target (the anchor) — the whole swarm's motion piles onto a single parameter; negative splays voices evenly across all four — motion smeared across the entire patch. At J=0 voices sit where the distribution scattered them. K still couples phase (voices modulate in step or independently). Sweep J and hear the modulation migrate through the patch as a body — vibrato collecting into one destination, then dispersing across all of them.
